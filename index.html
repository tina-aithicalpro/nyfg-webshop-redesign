Our Story

Cultivating the spirit of growth and change.

The New York Flower Group unifies three legacy houses into one standard of excellence.

The New York Flower Group brings together Dutch Flower Line, G. Page Wholesale Flowers, and A Rose By Harvest to provide the new standard of excellence in flowers. Located in the heart of the New York City Wholesale Flower Market, with our new 30,000 sq ft Long Island City facility, we supply the professional trade across the city and nationwide.

Our Heritage

Three legacy brands. One standard.

✽

Our Supply Chain

From farm to your studio, daily.

Fresh cut flowers arrive daily across all three locations. We source from local organic farms in our own backyard, and from international suppliers across the globe. Our buyers are on the Dutch auction floor weekly, bringing the newest varieties to the New York market before anyone else.
